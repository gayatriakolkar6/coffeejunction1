Our Story

Crafted with Passion Since 2015

Coffee Junction started as a small corner café with one simple dream — to serve the most comforting, flavourful cup of coffee in Pune. Today, we're a beloved destination for coffee lovers across the city.

Every bean we use is ethically sourced, every recipe is perfected over years of tasting, and every cup is made fresh just for you.

  • 100% Arabica & Robusta premium beans
  • Freshly brewed to order every time
  • Cozy ambiance — perfect for work or catchups
  • Vegan & allergen-friendly options available
